THE PROPOSAL

In October of 2014, Jared & Katie had the pleasure of visiting New York City for the first time in their lives. They had both wanted to visit the city for quite some time, and finally had the chance when Katie’s friend Lauren invited them out for a stay.

The forecast called for rain the morning they arrived, so with umbrellas in hand the trio headed to MOMA to spend an afternoon viewing masterpieces. Unfortunately, they weren’t the only one’s with this brilliant idea and first sight of the daunting line had them second guessing their plans. Instead they decided to backtrack to a quaint dinner they had passed on their walk, Lindy’s would be where they enjoyed lunch and waited out the rain.

Everyone was repeatedly checking their phones for weather updates, but sadly the rain wouldn’t be stopping until 5pm. Off they went to grab a drink and play some pool, which was the next best thing they could think to do. Then finally, the sky opened up and the rain came to a stop, Central Park was the next destination for this day of adventuring.

On the way there, Katie got distracted by a 99¢ pizza shop and almost derailed the whole plan. At this point it was starting to get dark and she was ready to call it a day, but Jared & Lauren pushed her onward and promised they’d grab a slice after the park. After making it and a short walk later, the three stopped on a quite little bridge where Katie asked Jared to take a picture of Lauren & her. This was the perfect set up for Jared to turn the table & have Lauren take a picture of Katie & himself.

Jared then handed Katie a present, which she unwrapped & found a photo album of the two of them. While Katie was looking at the album, Jared pulled out a little black box from his camera bag that he had been carrying around on his back all day. Jared then dropped to one knee and Katie without delay exclaimed “Oh my gosh, yes of course!”. All the while, Lauren was snapping pictures and captured the entire perfect moment.

Now engaged and ready to celebrate, off they went to get that long awaited slice!
